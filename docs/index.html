Environmental Economics

David Ubilava

September 2020

Preamble

This is a collection of notes designed to teach Environmental Economics at an undergraduate level. The content (loosely) follows Kolstad (2010) and Keohane and Olmstead (2016). Parts of these notes have benefited from teaching material generously shared with me by Matthew Interis, Leslie Martin, Ariel Ortiz-Bobea, David Stern, and Rebecca Taylor.

References

Keohane, Mr Nathaniel O, and Sheila M Olmstead. 2016. Markets and the Environment. Island Press.

Kolstad, Charles D. 2010. Environmental Economics. 2nd ed. Oxford University Press.
